Go High Level (GHL) is an all-in-one CRM and marketing automation platform that helps agencies and small businesses manage leads, pipelines, and campaigns in one place.

Why Choose HighLevel

HighLevel consolidates landing pages, forms, pipelines, email/SMS, and reporting under a single login, reducing subscription bloat and integration overhead.

Core Features

Pipelines & Deals

Visual pipelines to track lead stages, assign owners, and automate stage-based actions.

Conversations Inbox

Unified inbox for SMS, email, Facebook, Google, and WhatsApp so you never miss a message.

Automation

Powerful workflows for nurturing, appointment reminders, and lead qualification using triggers and conditions.

Reputation Management

Automate review requests and route negative feedback privately to protect your brand.

Getting Started

  1. Connect your domain, email, phone, and integrations.
  2. Import contacts and set up your first pipeline.
  3. Create a workflow for lead capture and instant follow‑up.

Best Practices

  • Keep one source of truth for contact data—avoid duplicating across tools.
  • Name stages and automations clearly for easy collaboration.
  • Track metrics weekly: new leads, reply rate, booked calls, close rate.

Conclusion

With a unified stack and robust automation, HighLevel helps teams move faster and convert more leads with less manual work.
