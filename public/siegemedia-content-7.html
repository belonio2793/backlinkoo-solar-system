Case Library: Patterns That Generalize

Marketplace

Inventory depth created confusion and crawl waste. Consolidated guides, canonicalized variants, and shipped a pricing index that drew citations. Paired with partial‑match anchors from relevant publications, category pages rose without aggressive exact‑match.

Fintech

Regulatory topics require precision. Built a terminology primer and calculators for fees/APY comparisons; journalists cited both in coverage. Equity flowed to product pages through careful internal linking.

Healthcare

Trust is paramount. Published clinician‑reviewed explainers with structured data and conservative anchors. Links clustered around patient education hubs that support service pages.

Outcomes and Leading Indicators

  • Discovery: rising impressions on broad topical queries
  • Depth: longer dwell time on explainers and playbooks
  • Authority: increased linking root domains to hubs and spokes
  • Revenue: assisted conversions from pages receiving editorial links
Annotate analytics with publication dates and anchor types to correlate movement with work product.
