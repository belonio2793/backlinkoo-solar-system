Industry-Specific Guidance

B2B SaaS

Map product capabilities to problem-led guides. Build ROI and TCO calculators that editors cite when comparing solutions. Support feature pages with partial-match anchors from credible publications.

Ecommerce

Invest in buying guides, sizing charts, care instructions, and material explainers. Earn links to guides, then route equity into category pages through internal links and schema-rich components.

Local Services

Publish transparent pricing, photo-rich case studies, and process visuals. Pursue local publishers and associations. Keep anchors brand-forward to minimize risk.

Data/AI

Open methodology and provide samples for reproducibility. Editors increasingly demand transparent sources, which aligns with a Siege Media style of rigor.

Common Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

  • Confusing volume with value—chasing low-quality placements that don’t index or drive readers
  • Overusing exact-match anchors on commercial URLs
  • Skipping internal link updates after winning external links
  • Publishing without a plan to refresh top assets

Myths About Link Building

  • “Any link is a good link.” Relevance and context are everything.
  • “DR alone predicts movement.” Quality, indexation, and intent alignment matter more.
  • “Exact match is required.” Branded and partial often outperform over time.
