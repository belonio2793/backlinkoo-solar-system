Open Tibia Servers: EvoluniaNet — A quick guide

Published September 21, 2025 • kyliecosmetics.org

Open Tibia (OT) servers remain a vibrant community for retro MMORPG enthusiasts. In this post we explore EvoluniaNet, a modern Open Tibia server known for balanced gameplay and community-driven events.

What is EvoluniaNet?

Evolunia is a custom Open Tibia server that focuses on accessibility for new players while offering depth for veteran players. The server provides regular updates, PvP arenas, and engaging quests.

Getting started

To join EvoluniaNet, download a compatible client and follow the server's connection guide. New players should check out the community forums and Discord to find helpful guides and starter packs.

Why players choose EvoluniaNet

  • Active moderation and community events
  • Balanced economy and item progression
  • Regular content updates from developers

Tips for new players

Focus on the main quests to unlock faster leveling, join a guild for social play, and participate in weekend events to earn exclusive rewards. Anchor links and organic content are great for discovering reliable guides.

Pro tip: Search the server forums for starter builds—many players share proven templates for leveling and equipment.

For more information, visit the official site: https://evolunia.net.
